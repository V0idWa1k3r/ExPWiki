Iron Plated Fired Brick

Iron Plated Fired Brick is a block added by ExPetrum.
It is obtained by crafting from a Fired Brick and a Double Iron Sheet.

Iron Plated Fired Brick is used as a part of a multiblock structure for Blast Furnace.
