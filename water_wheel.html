Water Wheel

Water Wheel is a block added by ExPetrum.
Water Wheel can be obtained by crafting from 8 Water Wheel Blades:

Water Wheel blades can be obtained by crafting from 3 Planks, a Saw and two Nails:

Water wheel generates rotary power when placed above water.
The amount of power it generates is based on the amound and levels of water blocks around it.
The formula is the following:
  • Speed: waterLevels * 16
  • Torque: waterLevels * 8
The water wheel will output power into a gearbox located directly behind it.
