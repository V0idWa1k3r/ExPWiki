Quern

Quern is a block added by ExPetrum.
Quern can be obtained by crafting from 3 rocks and 3 stones:

A Quern has an interface which can be opened by right-clicking the bottom half of it:

The slot on the top-left is for a grindstone. A grindstone can be crafted from a stick and 4 rocks:

The slot on the left is the input slot and the slot on the right is the output slot.
The player can perform a recipe by providing the quern with a grindstone, putting an item into the input slot and then right-clicking the top half of the quern block.
Each full rotation of the quern takes 1 durability from the grindstone and grindstone has 256 durability in total.
