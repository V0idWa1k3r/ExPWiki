Oven

Oven is a block added by ExPetrum.
Oven can be obtained by crafting from 8 Bricks and a Bronze Double Sheet:

Oven has an interface that can be opened by right-clicking:

The bar on the left is the temperature bar.
The fluid bar to the right of the temperature bar represents the current amount of Olive/Walnut Oil in the oven.
The slot on the top-left is for the Oli containers(example: Olive Oil Bottle).
The slot on the bottom-left is for the result item from consuming the fluid container item(example: Glass Bottle).
The four slots on the top are for the items that the player wishes to cook.
The slot directly underneath the four slots is for fuel.
The maximum temperature the oven can reach is 500 degrees.
The oven is unaffected by bellows.
The oven needs to be lit up in order to consume fuel and heat up. To lit the oven up it must be right-clicked with a Fire Starter or a Flint And Iron.
The maximum amount of Oil the oven can hold in itself is 1000mb.
The oven is intended for cooking. While it can heat up any item it will heat up food related items twice as fast(20 degrees/second).
Additionally the oven has the ability to apply the Well Cooked status to cooked food making it rot at 35% the rate.
To apply that effect the oven must have at least 50mb of Olive or Walnut oil in it while cooking the item.
Upon cooking the oven will consume 50mb of oil and apply the Well Cooked status to the food.
This mechanic only applies to food items.
