Pottery Station

Pottery Station is a block added by ExPetrum.
It is obtained by crafting from a stick and 3 rocks:

Pottery Station has an interface that can be opened with a right-click:

The slot on the left is for clay. Exactly 8 clay must be placed there.
The button with an image of an item allows the selection of the recipe. Clicking the button cycles through all available recipes.
In order to obtain the item the player must complete the minigame.
