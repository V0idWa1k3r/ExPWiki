Fired Brick

Fired Brick is a block added by ExPetrum.
Fired Brick block is obtained by crafting from 4 fired bricks.

Fired bricks are obtained by heating up fire clay to a temperature of 600 degrees(Red ☐☐☐☐)
Fire clay is obtained by crafting from clay, kaolin powder and flint powder:

Kaolin powder is obtained by grinding kaolin clay through a quern.
Flint powder is obtained by grinding flint through a quern.
Fired brick is used as a part of a multiblock structure for Forge and Bloomery
