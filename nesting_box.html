Nesting Box

Nesting Box is a block added by ExPetrum.
Nesting Box can be obtained by crafting from 3 Hay and 3 Planks

Nesting Box has an interface that can be opened by right-clicking the Nesting Box:

The purpose of nesting boxes is to collect Eggs from Chickens.
If a chicken trusts the player enough it will attempt to find a nesting box within a 16 block radius around itself to lay an egg in.
When a chicken lays an egg it ends up in the inventory of a nesting box provied that there is a slot for an egg.
Nesting boxes can also be used to incubate chickens from eggs.
If an egg is fertilized and at least 3 days have passed since the egg was layed the egg will be deleted and a new baby chicken will spawn on top of the nesting box.
Nesting boxes can hold up to 9 eggs at a time.
