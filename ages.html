Progression System

ExPetrum introduces a progression system that makes the player progress through Ages.
The progress through the ages is as following:
  1. Stone Age
  2. Chalcolithic Age
  3. Bronze Age
  4. Iron Age
  5. Rotary Age
  6. Steel Age
The progression through ages is usually triggered by crafting a tool of that age with some exceptions:
  • The player starts in a Stone age
  • Rotary age is triggered by crafting a gearbox
It is impossible to skip through ages. For example if a player managed to craft a bronze tool while still being in stone age the bronze age will not be triggered.
The ages are purely visual changing the way player's hotbar looks as well as changing some other minor visual icons.
However with the achievement of Iron Age the player is able to more accurately determine the amount of fluid in their watering cans and wooden buckets.
