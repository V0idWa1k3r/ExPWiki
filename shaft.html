Shafts

Shaft is a block added by ExPetrum.
Wooden Shaft can be obtained by crafting from 3 Planks and 4 Nails:

Alternatively Metal Shafts can be obtained by crafting from 2 Ingots and a Sheet of the metal in question:

Shafts are used to transmit rotary power from a gearbox to either another gearbox or to a power consumer.
A shaft has a maximum limit on how much rotary power it can transmit. If the power provided exceeds that limit the shaft will break(power = torque * speed).
The following materials have the following limits:
  • Wood: 2048
  • Copper: 8192
  • Bronze: 32768
  • Iron: 131072
  • Steel: 524288
  • HSLA Steel: 2097152
Additionally a shaft can only transmit power for a certain number of blocks. The limit depends on the material:
  • Wood: 3
  • Copper: 5
  • Bronze: 5
  • Iron: 7
  • Steel: 9
  • HSLA Steel: 12
