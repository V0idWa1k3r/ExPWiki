Bellows

Bellows is a block added by ExPetrum.
Bellows can be obtained by crafting from 3 Leather, a Copper Ingot and 4 Planks:

Bellows are used to increase the maximum temperature of temperature-containing blocks.
To do this a player must place the bellows so they face the block in question.
The bellows can then be right-clicked to increase the maximum temperature of the target block.
Manual Bellows typically increase the maximum temperature by 100 degrees up to the maximum of additional 500 degrees.
The increase in temperature in target block will slowly drop at a rate of 4 degrees per second.
