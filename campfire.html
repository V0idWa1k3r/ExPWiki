Campfire

Campgire is a block added by ExPetrum.
It is obtained by crafting from a stick, plant fiber and two rocks:

Campfire has an interface that can be opened with a right-click:

The bar on the left is a temperature indicator. If a campfire is lit and has fuel it will consume that fuel to raise the temperature.
The slot on the top is the item slot. Any item can be put in it and the campfire will heat that item up.
The 3 slots on the bottom are slots for fuel. Campfire will use the fuel to continue burning.
The maximum temperature the campfire can reach is 610 degrees.
Campfire is unaffected by bellows.
Campfire will drop as an item if the block it is placed on is not a solid block.
Campfire needs to be lit up in order to consume fuel and heat up. To lit the campfire up it must be right-clicked with a Fire Starter or a Flint And Iron.
Campfire will emit fire particles if it is lit up.
