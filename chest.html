Chest

Chest is a block added by ExPetrum.
Chest can be obtained by crafting from 8 Planks and a Lock

Chest has an interface that can be opened by right-clicking:

Unlike vanilla chests chests added by ExPetrum can be placed next to eachother and they don't connect.
The chests can be used as a mid-game storage.
