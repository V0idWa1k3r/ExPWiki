Thirst System

Thirst

ExPetrum introduces a new thirst mechanic into the game. The player has 3000 thirst(max and base).
The thirst gets rediced by the following formula:
  • If time was skipped(player slept): skippedTicks * 0.0625
  • Otherwise: 0.125 * exhaustion
If the thirst is less than or equal to 0 the player takes 1 point of Starvation damage every 2 seconds.
The thirst is displayed in the bottom-left of the screen and the text roughly represents the percentage of thirst the player has left:
  • Not Thirsty: 100% - 83%
  • Slightly Thirsty: 83% - 66%
  • Thirsty: 66% - 50%
  • Very Thirsty: 50% - 33%
  • Extremely Thirsty: 33% - 16%
  • Dying Of Dehydration: 16% - 0%
Thirst can be replenished by drinking water puddles/blocks(200 thirst per sip), drinking from jugs with water(1000 per jug) or by drinking fluid bottles with either juice, milk or water(250 per bottle)
