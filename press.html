Fruit Press

Fruit Press is a block added by ExPetrum.
Fruit Press can be obtained by crafting from 3 Planks and a Copper Double Sheet

Fruit Press has an interface that can be opened by right-clicking:

The press is used to squeese items usually getting fluid as a byproduct.
To perform a recipe the player must insert an item into the only slot of the press.
Additionally a fluid container of any kind(example: Barrel) must be placed directly underneath the press.
Then a player must jump on the top of the press multiple times.
If a fluid can be inserted into the fluid container underneath then the press will perform the recipe.
