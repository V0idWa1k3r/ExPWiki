Wind System

ExPetrum introduces the wind to the world.
Wind periodically changes direction and strength.
The wind is displayed in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
The first letter in the text indicates the direction the wind blows in.
After that the text displays the strength of the wind:
  • Calm: Less than 1 km/h
  • Light Air: 1 ~ 5 km/h
  • Light Breeze: 5 ~ 11 km/h
  • Gentle Breeze: 11 ~ 19 km/h
  • Moderate Breeze: 19 ~ 28 km/h
  • Fresh Breeze: 28 ~ 38 km/h
  • Fresh Wind: 38 ~ 49 km/h
  • Strong Wind: 49 ~ 61 km/h
  • Fresh Gale: 61 ~ 74 km/h
  • Strong Gale: 74 ~ 88 km/h
  • Whole Gale: 88 ~ 102 km/h
  • Storm: 102 ~ 117 km/h
  • Hurricane: More than 117 km/h
The speed of the wind affects the efficiency of the windmill and the speed of falling leaves particles.
The direction of the wind affects the direction of falling leaves particles.
