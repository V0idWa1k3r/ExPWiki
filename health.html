Health System

Health


In ExPetrum health works differently to how it works in vanilla.
Your maximum and base health is 5000 instead of 20. However all received damage is multiplied accordingly.
The amount of health you have is represented by the heart icon in the bottom left of your screen.
The text varies and represents the rough percentage of health you currently have:
  • Healthy: 100% - 83%
  • Injured: 83% - 66%
  • Heavily Injured: 66% - 50%
  • Dying: 50% - 33%
  • Nearly Dead: 33% - 16%
  • Dead: 16% - 0%

Health Regen


The player slowly regenerates health at a rate of 10hp per 5 seconds
or
if time was skipped(for example the player slept) then health regen is calculated for the time skipped by this formula:
5 * (ticksSkipped / 100).
Currently health regen is unaffected by thirst, hunger or illnesses.
