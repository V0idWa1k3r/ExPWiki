Splitter Gearbox

Splitter Gearbox is a block added by ExPetrum.
Splitter Gearbox can be obtained by crafting from 4 Nails, 2 Planks and 3 Gear Assemblies.

A splitter gearbox is used to split the input power evenly between 2 outputs.
A splitter gearbox has one input side and 2 output sides marked by specific textures.
When a player places a splitter gearbox the input side will be facing the player and 2 output faces will be to the left and to the right of the input face.
The output faces can be changed by either right-clicking any side of the splitter gearbx or shift-right clicking any side of the splitter gearbox.
The splitter gearbox will split power evenly. That means that if it is receiving 10 power it will output 5 units of power to each output face even if nothing is connected to them.
