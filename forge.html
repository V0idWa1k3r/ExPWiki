Forge

Forge is a multiblock structure.
Its purpose is to heat up items to a much higher temperatures than a campfire could.
To build the forge the player will need:
  • 1 Forge Core
  • 15 Fire Bricks
  • Up to 40 any solid blocks

And then your forge is assembled. To work with it open it's interface with a right click.
The bottom 5 slots are for fuel.
The top 5 slots are for items the player wants to heat up.
Inserting some fuel into the forge and right clicking it with a fire starter or a flint and iron will activate the forge.
