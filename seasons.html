Seasons System

World Seasons

In ExPetrum the world has 4 seasons it cycles through. Each season is different in both temperature and the effects of the season.
The following seasons exist:
  • Spring
  • Summer
  • Autumn
  • Winter
The current season is displayed in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
Each season by default lasts 30 in-game days.
Each season has varying temperatures:
  • Spring:
    • Early: -2 ~ +4
    • Middle: +4 ~ +13
    • End: +12 ~ +18
  • Summer:
    • Early: +14 ~ +25
    • Middle: +25 ~ +33
    • End: +15 ~ +33
  • Autumn:
    • Early: +2 ~ +28
    • Middle: -1 ~ +9
    • End: -14 ~ +3
  • Winter:
    • Early: -29 ~ -9
    • Middle: -23 ~ -13
    • End: -12 ~ -2
Different crops can grow during different seasons. Most crops however grow during all seasons but the winter.
During a season the appearance of grass, leaves and bushes might change.
During spring or summer leaves, grass and bushes will go into their normal state.
During autumn leaves, grass and bushes will go into their autumn state. More falling leaves particles will also spawn.
During winter leaves, grass and bushes will go into their dead state.
Additionally the state of leaves, grass and bushes generated in the world will match the season.
