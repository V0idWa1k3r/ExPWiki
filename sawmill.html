Sawmill

Sawmill is a block added by ExPetrum.
Sawmill can be obtained by crafting from 4 Iron Double Sheets, a Gear Assembly and a Saw Blade:

Sawmill is used to process logs into planks and wooden pulp.
In order to work a Sawmill must be supplied with rotary power.
The Sawmill requires a minimum of 64 speed and torque.
The Sawmill accepts power from the Sides.
The speed at which the Sawmill processes logs is calculated by the following formula:
speed + torque
In order for a log to be processed it needs to be placed directly underneath the sawmill.
Once the progress reaches 100% the log will be broken and 4 planks will be dropped alongside with 2 wooden pulps.
