Spinning Wheel

Spinning Wheel is a block added by ExPetrum.
Spinning Wheel can be obtained by crafting from 3 Sticks and 4 Planks

Spinning wheel is used to create Wool Yarn
First the player must right-click the spinning wheel with the Carded Wool
Then the player must right-click the spinning wheel 10 times.
Finally the Wool Yarn can be removed from the spinning wheel by right-clicking.
