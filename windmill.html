Windmill

Windmill is a block added by ExPetrum.
Windmill can be obtained by crafting from 4 Windmill Blades and a Gear Assembly:

Windmill blades can be obtained by crafting from 3 Planks, 2 Treated Sticks and 4 Nails:

Windmill generates power based on the speed of wind.
The formula is the following:
  • Speed: wind * 16
  • Torque: wind * 4
The windmill will output power into a gearbox located directly behind it.
