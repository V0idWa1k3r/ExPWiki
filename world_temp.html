World Temperature System

World Temperature

In ExPetrum the temperature of the world changes over time unlike in vanilla where the temperature is based on the biome only.
Similar to vanilla each biome also has it's temperature value. The final temperature is calculated as:
biomeTemperature + worldTemperature
The temperature is displayed in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
The text corresponds to the current temperature in celcius:
  • Freezing Cold: Less than -30 degrees
  • Very Cold: -30 to -20
  • Cold: -20 to -12
  • Slightly Cold: -12 to -2
  • Water Freezing: -2 to +2
  • Warm: +2 to +12
  • Very Warm: +12 to +20
  • Hot: +20 to +30
  • Insanely Hot: More than +30 degrees
The temperature display takes the temperature of the biome you are currently in into consideration.

Temperature Effects

The temperature of the world has the following effects:
  • If the temperature is below 0 the water will start freezing and instead of rain snow will fall and accumulate.
  • If the temperature is below 0 the snow will create snow layers in the world
  • If the temperature is above 0 the rain will create water puddles
  • If the temperature is above 0 snow layers and water puddles will slowly dissapate
  • Crop health and growth rate is affected by temperature

Temperature Settings

Temperature is randomly generated at the beginning of each day.
The initial values for temperature are obtained from ExPetrum.jar/assets/exp/data/seasonsTemperatureRanges.json
