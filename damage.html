Damage and Armor Systems

Body Parts

In ExPetrum the player's body is divided into the following body parts:
  • Head: Protected by: Helmet
  • Body: Protected by: Chestplate
  • Left Arm: Protected by: Chestplate
  • Right Arm: Protected by: Chestplate
  • Left Leg: Protected by: Leggings, Boots
  • Right Leg: Protected by: Leggings, Boots

Damage

The damage calculation steps for the player are as following:
  1. At first a body part to damage is determined based on the source:
    • Anvil damage has a 92% chance of damaging the head and 8% chance of damaging the body.
    • Falling damage has a 95% chance of damaging either of the legs and a 5% chance of damaging the body
    • Crushing(falling block) damage has a 37% chance of damaging the head, 55% chance of damaging ether the body, the left arm or the right arm and a 8% chance of damaging either leg.
    • Hot floor(magma blocks) damage can damage either of the legs
    • Any other damage source will randomly select a body part to damage
  2. Based on the body part selected an armor damage reduction is applied.
  3. The damage to the body part is then applied. It is a random value [0-1] not based on the actual damage received. If no armor protection was applied that value is multiplied by 10.
  4. The actual damage is calculated by the following formula: amount * (200 +/-[0~50])
  5. The health of the player is modified as currentHealth - damage
  6. If the health of the player is less than or equal to 0 then the player is killed

Armor Protection

Currently the armor protection for non-special mod armor only takes armor toughness attribute into account when calculation the amount of damage reduced.
Armor is damaged for half the damage received.
