Combat System

ExPetrum introduces a custom combat system into the game. Different key combinations with various items may produce special attacks.

Weights and Weapons

ExPetrum introduces a weapon weight system. Each weapon has an associated weight and a weapon type:
Weapon weights:
  • Light
  • Normal
  • Heavy
Weapon types:
  • Sword
  • Dagger
  • Spear
  • Axe
  • Hammer
  • Battleaxe(Axe + Sword)
  • Halberd(Battleaxe + Spear)
  • Battlehammer(Hammer)
  • Scythe(Sword)
  • None
Weapon stats:
  • Battleaxe: Heavy, Battleaxe
  • Chisel: Light, None
  • Gardening Tool: Light, None
  • Hammer: Heavy, Hammer
  • Hoe: Heavy, None
  • Knift: Light, Dagger
  • Pickaxe: Heavy, None
  • Saw: Normal, None
  • Scythe: Heavy, Scythe
  • Shovel: Heavy, None
  • Spear: Normal, Spear
  • Sword: Normal, Sword

Special Attacks

The following special attacks exist:
  • Behead:
    • Triggered by: Pressing D(right) and Left Mouse Button(attack)
    • Executed by: Normal or Heavy Axes
    • Effect: 100% attack damage, 1 - targetHealth / targetMaxHealth * 10% chance of instakill.
  • Down Strike:
    • Triggered by: Pressing S(back), W(forward) and Left Mouse Button(attack)
    • Executed by: Normal or Heavy Hammers, Axes or Swords
    • Effect: Strong knockback, stuns the target for a second(30 ticks with a heacy weapon)
  • Throw:
    • Triggered by: Pressing S(back), W(forward) and Left Mouse Button(attack)
    • Executed by: Light Daggers
    • Effect: Throws the currently equipped weapon in the direction of the attack. Thrown weapon deals 100% damage and passes through targets.
  • Piercing Dash:
    • Triggered by: Sprinting and Pressing Left Mouse Button(attack)
    • Executed by: Any Swords, Spears and Daggers
    • Effect: Lunges the player forward dealing (1 - distance / 6) * 100% damage to all enemies in a line in front of the player. Also applies strong knockback to all targets.
  • Shield Slam:
    • Triggered by: Pressing W(forward), blocking(right mouse button) and Left Mouse Button(attack)
    • Executed by: Shields
    • Effect: Strong knockback to the target.
  • Slash:
    • Triggered by: Pressing S(back) and Left Mouse Button(attack)
    • Executed by: Any not Daggers or Spears
    • Effect: 85% attack damage to every enemy in front of the player.
  • Spin:
    • Triggered by: Pressing D(right), A(left) and Left Mouse Button(attack)
    • Executed by: Normal or Heavy Swords, Hammers and Axes
    • Effect: 75% attack damage to all enemies around the player. Physically spins the player.
  • Stab:
    • Triggered by: Pressing S(back) and Left Mouse Button(attack)
    • Executed by: Light Daggers
    • Effect: A flurry of 10 stabs to the target in front of the player ignoring invincibility frames each stab dealing 10% damage
