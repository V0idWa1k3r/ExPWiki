Mechanical Pottery Station

Mechanical Pottery Station is a block added by ExPetrum.
It is obtained by crafting from 2 treated sticks, a gear assembly and two Double Iron Sheets:

Mechanical Pottery Station has an interface that can be opened with a right-click:

The slot on the left is for clay. Any amount of clay greater than 7 must be placed there.
The button with an image of an item allows the selection of the recipe. Clicking the button cycles through all available recipes.
The slot on the right is the result slot. As the pottery station performs the recipe the result can be taken from that slot.
Mechanical pottery station is a device that requires Rotary Power. It can accept Rotary Power from Below.
The speed of the mechanical pottery station is calculated by the following forumla:
speed + torque / 8
