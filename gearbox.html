Gearbox

Gearbox is a block added by ExPetrum.
Gearbox can be obtained by crafting from 4 Nails, 4 Planks and a Gear Assembly.

A gearbox is used to transmit rotary power. It has 2 specific sides marked by specific textures - the input and the output.
When a player places the gearbox the output side will be facing the player and the input side will be facing the opposite direction.
The player can change the input side by right-clicking on any face of the gearbox.
Shift-clicking on a face of a gearbox will set the input side to the opposite face.
Gearboxes can accept power from both shafts and power producing blocks.
Gearboxes can't output the power to anything but shafts.
