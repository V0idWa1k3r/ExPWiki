Trough

Trough is a block added by ExPetrum.
Quern can be obtained by crafting from 5 Planks and 3 sticks:

A trough can store up to 1000mb(1 bucket) of water.
Water can be inserted into the trough by right-clicking it with a wooden bucket.
Any item that's heated up can be right-clicked on the trough to instantly cool it down to 0 consuming 100mb of water from the trough.
Additionally any item that was smithed can be cooled down on the trough by right-clicking. That not only sets the heat to 0 but restores some integrity and removes all the heat(read more)
