Scraping Rack

Scraping Rack is a block added by ExPetrum.
Scraping Rack can be obtained by crafting from 4 Sticks and a Plank:

Scraping Rack is used to scrape soaked hides.
A Soaked Hide may be placed on a scraping rack by right-clicking.
The player then must right-click the rack with the hide with any knife 10 times.
Then a Prepared Hide can be obtained by right-clicking the scraping rack.
